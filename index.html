Bring your own bottle

Your wine. Our food.
Everyone wins.

We're a BYOB eatery — bring your favourite bottle of wine or beer and we'll take care of the glasses. You pay a small corkage instead of restaurant drink prices, which usually saves a table of four more than the cost of a main.

1Grab your favourite bottle — from the shop, or your own cellar.
2Book a table or walk in, Tuesday to Sunday from 5pm.
3We chill it, pour it and add a small corkage to the bill. That's it.

Our story

From Nizam, with love

Curry Kitchen was opened by Salim — the man behind Nizam, the restaurant East Grinstead trusted for years. When Nizam's chapter closed, the recipes, the spice blends and the standards didn't go anywhere: they moved to 194 London Road.

Everything is cooked to order, the way it was at Nizam. The only difference? Now you can enjoy it at home — or eat in with your own bottle of wine.
